Projects

Stock Market Data Analysis & Stock Price Prediction

Objective: To make an attempt at predicting the stock market by using the ANN (Artificial Neural Network) methodology.

Approach: Extracted past six months data of five companies from the CMIE website. Reduced the multidimensionality using PCA and used it as an input vector for ANN.

Results: The resulting model outperformed and predicted the prices of stocks with an accuracy of more than 97%.

Medical Insurance Cost Prediction

Objective: To accurately predict the medical insurance charges of a person based on categorical and continuous features of the dataset.

Approach: Visualized & preprocessed the dataset by normalizing it and then dividing it into a 20% training & 80% testing dataset. Employed & compared different models on training dataset.

Results: Achieved an accuracy of 90.71% on the testing dataset using the Gradient Boosting Regressor algorithm.
